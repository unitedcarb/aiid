Weekly Design Marketing Updates

Lynda.com 2018 Business Strategy: 60% Investment in Adobe Content
A team of PMM and PMs met onsite with Lynda.com content managers last week. In 2018, Lynda.com is investing 60% of tutorials to support Adobe products. Of the Adobe products, Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign represent the top 3 viewerships in Lynda.com’s portfolio. Also noted, Lynda.com also features tutorials for product releases. These tutorials are called “Adobe Day + Date” updates.
View the full presentation from Lynda.com here

#AiLoveYou Valentines Campaign Launches This Week: Design Challenge
In celebration of Valentines on 2/14, social media is launching #AiLoveYou campaign. Users are invited to participate in a challenge by sharing their Valentine designs to Adobe Illustrator’s Twitter or Facebook by Monday, February 12, for the chance to win a one-year subscription to Creative Cloud. The winner will be announced on Wednesday, February 14.
View the blog post for #AiLoveYou campaign here

New InDesign Prerelease: PS/AI Shortcuts, Export Single Page PDFs, Mobile Presets
InDesign issued a new prerelease last week (InDesign CC 2018.1.60). With this prerelease you can use Photoshop and Illustrator shortcuts in InDesign; Export document as separate PDF pages; and access New Document presets for iPhone X and Samsung S8. Prerelease can be downloaded at www.adobeprerelease.com.
View the full prerelease documentation here

Japan Price Promotions Begin This Week: Single App & All App
The planned Japan price promotion begins on 2/5 to 3/2. Under the price promotion Single App will be 1280 yen (equivalent to $11.31 USD) and All Apps Stock Bundle will be 3980 yen (equivalent to $35.17 USD).
View more details about the price promotion here

Illustrator Mentioned In 2018 Type Trends Article With Dan Rhatigan
The Dieline (UVM: 256,982) posted their feature with Dan Rhatigan discussing his POV on type trends for 2018. The piece positions him as a type industry thought leader, including his commentary around what influences type trends, his favorite type trend of 2017, and type predictions for 2018. Dan also recommends several useful tools for developing new typefaces, highlighting Illustrator CC for constructing type forms and Glyphs and Robofont for font development and production.
Read the Dieline article here

YouTube Experiment: Paid Display Ads To Videos Drives Engagement
The paid media team experimented with paid display ads in YouTube to drive views to Adobe videos. The results show that driving users to Adobe YouTube video ultimately increased user engagement. Users who were exposed to the test saw an increase in average Customer Engagement Index of 1% Post-Launch. This is 3 percentage points higher than control group change (-2%).
