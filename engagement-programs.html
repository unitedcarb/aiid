Choose Your Own Adventure Running Ask users what they want to learn will allow us to show them more relevant content. Rather than assuming we know what users want to learn, can we ask them what they're interested in (graphic design, logo, print design, etc.) and show them tutorials, assets, and templates directly related. By engaging users earlier about what they want to learn, we can help keep them engaged. Gather data from clicks of emails to optimize Illustrator emails.   Reference
